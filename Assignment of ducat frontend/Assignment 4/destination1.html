About Paris


Paris, known as the City of Light, is famous for its art, fashion, and culture. Visit iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre, and Notre-Dame Cathedral.

Explore charming neighborhoods like Montmartre and Le Marais, and enjoy world-class dining experiences.
