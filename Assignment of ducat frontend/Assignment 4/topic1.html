About the Solar System


The Solar System consists of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, including eight planets, their moons, and various other celestial bodies.

Key Facts:

  • Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun.
  • Jupiter is the largest planet in the Solar System.
  • Saturn has prominent ring structures.
